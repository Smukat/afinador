Regulation and Tuning of the piano

The new book of Jean-Marie Paul/h3>

Jean-Marie Paul, Professor of “Tuning and Regulation” at the Conservatory of Music of Malaga, provides in this book a clear, accessible and reader-friendly approach to those seeking to learn the foundations of the piano regulation and the theory of tuning.

The purpose of this book is to help pianists to acquire a deeper knowledge of their instrument, both theoretical and pragmatic. It will provide the tools and the confidence that are necessary to solve most of the challenges that may arise during their careers.
